Who am I?

I’m a Data Scientist and Analyst with a strong foundation in Mathematics and Statistics. My core focus is Data Analytics and AI Automation — building systems that move beyond reports and actually power day-to-day decision-making.

I work end-to-end: from cleaning and structuring messy datasets, to designing analytical models, to deploying automation pipelines that run reliably in production.
